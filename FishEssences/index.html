    Runes¶

    The fish you catch aren't just for show - they're juicy with fish essences that can be squeezed into your gear to empower them, acting as runes.

    Every fish is full of oils that can be used as a rune.

    Each fish species provides different bonuses when equipped, and higher-tier fish provide stronger bonuses.

    You can only equip fish essences to "body" slots: helmet, chest, leggings, belt, boots, and gloves.

    Upgrading Fish Essences¶

    The power of fish essences starts out weak, as a single fish has little density of essence. To draw out more power, you can combine 4 fish of the same species to gain stronger bonuses. Rarer fish may also gain additional perks when upgraded!

    Combining 4 of the same fish upgrades its essence.

    Combining fish creates a new one of a higher tier, with tier 3 being the maximum. To upgrade to the next tier, you must combine fishes of the same tier.

    Higher-rarity fish require Loremaster to identify before they can be used:

    Rarity Loremaster required
    Common, Uncommon 0
    Rare 1
    Epic 2
    Legendary 3

    Combined fish require higher Loremaster to identify, with each tier adding +1 to the requirement. You can however equip fish essences even if unidentified.

    Examples¶

    Here are a few examples to give you a sense of what fish runes do:

    • Sardine — A steady Common fish. Straightforward bonuses at all tiers.
    • Starfish — An Uncommon fish with high endurance. Its rune reflects its resilience.
    • Pearl — A challenging Rare. Its rune offers a noticeably stronger bonus than common catches.

    A full rune list is intentionally omitted here to preserve the discovery experience. It will be added in a future update.

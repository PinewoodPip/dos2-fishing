    Skills¶

    Fishermancy skills are combat skills unlocked as your Fishermancy level grows. Each one requires a minimum Fishermancy level and at least one point in a specific paired school.

    Seasick¶

    Seasick is a recurring status effect applied by several Fishermancy skills. Understanding it upfront makes the skill descriptions easier to follow.

    A character afflicted with Seasick:

    • Loses their turn when hit by water damage.
    • Has reduced Water Resistance and Air Resistance.

    Seasick makes enemies vulnerable to follow-up water and air attacks, and creates dangerous synergies with skills like With the Currents or Blow the Hornpipe.

    Reel In¶

    Paired school: Warfare**

    Pull a target toward you. The target takes piercing damage for each meter traveled - the further they fly, the more it hurts. Pull distance and damage both scale with Fishermancy.

    Deploy Fishnets¶

    Paired school: Huntsman**

    Place between one and three traps on the ground. Traps take one turn to arm. When an enemy steps on an armed trap:

    • They take damage, become Slowed, and a web surface is created.
    • You gain Trawling Grindset, which enables reaction attacks against enemies caught in your nets.

    Prepare Sashimi¶

    Paired school: Scoundrel**

    Execute a low-health enemy that is afflicted with Seasick. On success, you gain Slashimi — a buff that applies Seasick to enemies once per turn on basic attacks.

    Blue Fireball¶

    Paired school: Pyrokinetic**

    A projectile of volatile blue fire. Deals fire damage and applies Seasick on hit. Creates a cursed water surface in an area on impact, combining fire and water for volatile results.

    Blow the Hornpipe¶

    Paired school: Hydrosophist**

    A shout that grants nearby allies Horned Pipe Hype:

    • Increased Water Resistance.
    • Restores vitality and armor on critical hits. The amount restored scales with Fishermancy.

    Blast Cannon Ball¶

    Paired school: Geomancer**

    Launch a cannonball that pushes targets backward on impact. Targets take bonus damage if they slam into a wall. The cannonball can bounce, potentially hitting multiple enemies. Push distance scales with Fishermancy.

    With the Currents¶

    Paired school: Aerotheurge**

    Summon a ring of fish that encircle you. Fish bite enemies who move too close, dealing damage and applying Seasick. Also creates a cursed water surface in the area.

    Summon Swashbuckler¶

    Paired school: Necromancy**

    Summon an undead swashbuckler who fights with a knife and knows several combat skills. While the swashbuckler is active, nearby allies gain Swashbuckling Spirit, granting bonus weapon damage.

    Return to Crab¶

    Paired school: Polymorph**

    Transform yourself into Crab Form. While a crab, you are immune to all surfaces. You gain access to a set of crab-specific skills:

    • Crab Pinch & Punch — A melee attack.
    • Breath of Fresh Sea Air — A short-range breath attack.
    • Burrow — Dive underground briefly.
    • Crab Dance — A dance that grants stat bonuses.
    • Return to Modernity — Exit Crab Form.

    Turn on The Tides¶

    Paired school: Summoning**

    Buff all of your active summons with Turned On Tides. At the start of each affected summon's turn, they release a burst of water that damages and knocks back nearby enemies.
